The Old Page pain point

The page is very empty.



The 404 page Purpose

To help or lead users to find out what they are looking for.

To suggest users the new assets.

The New Page

The New Error page not just show the users they input a wrong link and also shows them the options.



Section 1: Let users know they used a wrong link to get into this page and it is possible to be back to the home page.

Section 2: Lead the users to pick the topic of the products what they may look for.

Section 3: Suggest some quick links to the asset pages for more information.

Section 4: Talk to the employee to help them out.

Test Result



The test ran 60 days with 5598 visits and 1637 clicks.

According to the result, the 404 page is important to the users too.
